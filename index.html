Frequently Asked Questions

Do you store my subscription data in the cloud?

No. All your subscription data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal subscription information.

Can I use MySubs on multiple devices?

Currently, data is stored locally on each device. If you purchase Premium, you can restore your purchase on other devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

What happens if I cancel Premium?

You'll retain Premium access until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll return to the free tier (5 subscription limit) and notifications will be disabled. Your subscription data will not be deleted.

How are notifications scheduled?

Notifications are sent at 10:00 AM based on your chosen timing (same day, 1 day, 3 days, or 1 week before renewal).

Will there be more features?

Yes! We're working on widgets and other features that will be available to Premium subscribers.
